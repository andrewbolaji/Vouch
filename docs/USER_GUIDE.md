# Vouch User Guide

Your guide to finding, saving, and voting on the best restaurants in your city.

---

## Welcome to Vouch

Vouch is a curated list of the Top 10 restaurants in every city, ranked by the people who actually live there. No algorithms, no paid placements. Just locals voting for the places they love.

Each city has one list. Ten spots. If a restaurant is on the list, locals put it there.

---

## Getting started

When you first open Vouch, you will see a short walkthrough with three screens:

1. **Curated, not crowdsourced.** Every city gets a Top 10, ranked by locals.
2. **Your vote matters.** Upvote your favorites and the rankings shift.
3. **Unlock insider access.** Get the full Top 10, insider tips, and save your favorites.

You can tap **Next** to move through the screens, or tap **Skip** in the top right to go straight to the app. You only see this walkthrough once.

---

## Finding restaurants

### The home screen

After the walkthrough, you land on the home screen. You will see:

- The Vouch logo and tagline at the top.
- A search bar to filter cities by name or state.
- A grid of city cards, each showing the city name, state, and a short description.

Tap any city card to see that city's restaurant rankings.

### Searching for a city

Tap the search bar and start typing. The city grid updates as you type. To clear your search, delete the text in the search bar. The full list comes back.

If no cities match your search, you will see "We're not there yet" with a note to suggest a city from your profile.

### City rankings

When you tap a city, you see the Top 5 restaurants by default. Each restaurant card shows:

- The restaurant's rank (1 through 10)
- Name, cuisine type, and price level ($ through $$$$)
- A photo

At the top of the list, you can switch between **Top 5** and **Top 10** using the toggle buttons.

- **Top 5** is available to everyone.
- **Top 10** requires a Locals Pass or City Insider membership. If you are on the free plan, tapping Top 10 shows a preview with the rank numbers but not the restaurant details, along with an option to upgrade.

### Restaurant details

Tap any restaurant card to see the full details:

- A large photo at the top.
- The restaurant's rank, name, cuisine, and price level.
- Vibe tags (like "Date Night," "Late Night," "Cash Only") if available.
- A vote button and a save button (more on those below).
- A description of the restaurant.
- Locations with addresses, if the restaurant has multiple spots.
- Insider notes (City Insider members only).
- A comments section.

You can share a restaurant by tapping the share icon in the top right corner.

---

## Saving favorites

Tap the bookmark icon on any restaurant detail page to save it. The icon fills in to show it is saved. Tap it again to remove it.

Saving requires a Locals Pass or City Insider membership. If you are on the free plan, tapping the bookmark icon opens the upgrade screen.

To see all your saved restaurants, go to your profile and tap **Saved Restaurants**. Your saves are grouped by city. Tap any saved restaurant to go back to its detail page.

If you have not saved anything yet, you will see "No saved restaurants yet" with a note that your favorites will appear there once you save them.

---

## Voting and commenting

### Voting

On any restaurant detail page, tap the vote button (the upward arrow with the vote count) to cast your vote. You must be signed in to vote. If you are not signed in, tapping the vote button opens the sign-in screen.

The button changes color to show your vote is counted, and the number updates right away. Tap it again to remove your vote. You get one vote per restaurant.

### Commenting

Scroll down on any restaurant detail page to find the comments section. Type your comment in the text field and tap the send button.

To reply to someone, tap the reply option on their comment. A banner appears showing who you are replying to. Type your reply and tap send. To cancel a reply, tap the X on the reply banner.

Comments have a 500-character limit. If no one has commented yet, you will see "No comments yet. Be the first."

If you are a City Insider member, your comments show an insider badge.

## How rankings work

<!-- TODO: screenshot of a city ranking list showing rank badges -->

### The short version

Every restaurant's rank comes from local votes. The list updates once a day. Recent votes count more than old ones, so the rankings reflect where locals are eating now, not where they were eating a year ago.

### What happens when you vote

When you tap the vote button, two things happen at different speeds:

1. **The vote count updates right away.** You see the number go up (or down if you remove your vote) the moment you tap.
2. **The rank position settles in the next daily update.** Rankings are recalculated every morning at 6 AM UTC. Your vote is included in that next run. So if you vote at 2 PM, the rank will reflect your vote by the following morning.

### Why recent votes count more

Vouch uses a system called time decay. A vote cast today has its full weight. A vote from three months ago counts for about half. A vote from six months ago counts for about a quarter. This keeps the list alive: a restaurant that stops earning new votes gradually drifts down, and a restaurant that locals are excited about right now can climb.

The half-life is 90 days. That means every 90 days, a vote's influence is cut in half.

### New cities and starting order

When a new city launches, it starts with a curated order based on local research. Behind the scenes, that starting order is backed by a set of initial votes that give the ranking engine something to work with on day one. Those starter votes fade naturally over the following months as real votes come in. After a few months, the list is shaped almost entirely by the community.

The large vote number shown next to each restaurant is a display figure that counts every vote ever cast. The ranking engine looks at the individual vote records and their ages, not this total, when it decides the order.

### Limits and things to know

- **Ranks change at most once a day.** The list does not jump around in real time. Everyone sees the same stable order all day, and it updates overnight.
- **One vote per person per restaurant.** You cannot vote twice for the same place. This is enforced by your account, not your device, so switching phones does not give you extra votes.
- **Verified-visit weighting is not live yet.** A future update will let City Insider members who verify they visited a restaurant have their vote count for more. For now, every vote counts equally.

### Where rankings show up

- **The Top 5 list** on the city screen (visible to all users).
- **The Top 10 list** on the city screen (Locals Pass and City Insider members).
- **The rank badge** on each restaurant's detail page, showing its position in the city.

## Insider tips

City Insider members see an extra section on restaurant detail pages called **Insider Notes**. This includes:

- **What to order:** A specific dish recommendation from someone who knows the restaurant well.
- **Pro tip:** Practical advice, like the best time to go or how to skip the wait.

If you are not a City Insider, this section shows a blurred preview with a lock icon and an option to upgrade.

## Membership

Vouch has three plans:

### Free
- See the Top 5 restaurants in every city.
- Vote on your favorites.
- Leave comments.

### Locals Pass ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- See the full Top 10.
- Save restaurants to your favorites.
- Trending tab.
- No ads.

### City Insider ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Everything in Locals Pass.
- Insider "what to order" tips.
- Insider badge on your comments.
- Your votes count double.
- Early access when new cities launch.

To upgrade, go to your profile and tap **Upgrade Plan**. You can switch between monthly and yearly billing using the toggle at the top. Each plan has a **Start 7-day free trial** button.

If you have purchased a plan on another device, tap **Restore purchases** at the bottom of the upgrade screen.

## Your profile

Tap the profile icon in the top right corner of the home screen. Your profile shows:

- Your name and current plan.
- **Sign In** (if you are not signed in yet).
- **Saved Restaurants** with a count of how many you have saved.
- **Upgrade Plan** to see membership options.
- **Notifications** to control which alerts you receive.
- **Share App** to send Vouch to a friend.
- **About** to see the app version and contact info.
- **Sign Out** (if you are signed in).
- **Delete Account** (if you are signed in). See "Deleting your account" below.

### Signing in

Tap **Sign In** from your profile. You can sign in with:

- **Google** (tap "Continue with Google")
- **Apple** (tap "Continue with Apple")
- **Email** (enter your email and password, then tap "Sign In")

If you are new, tap "New here? Create an account" at the bottom. You will need your name, email, and a password. Passwords must be at least 6 characters.

If something goes wrong while signing in, you will see a message explaining what happened and what to try next. For example: "Wrong email or password. Please try again." or "No account found with that email. Check the spelling or create a new account."

If your connection is slow or unavailable, you will see: "Couldn't reach Vouch. Check your connection and try again."

### Deleting your account

If you are signed in, you can delete your account from the profile screen. Tap **Delete Account**. A confirmation will ask if you are sure. Tap **Delete** to confirm.

This permanently deletes your account. It cannot be undone. If you change your mind, you can always create a new account.

### Notifications

From your profile, tap **Notifications**. You can turn these on or off:

- **Ranking changes:** Get notified when a restaurant you voted for moves up or down.
- **New cities:** Be the first to know when a new city launches.
- **Weekly digest:** A weekly summary of ranking changes across your saved cities.

---

## Suggesting changes

At the bottom of your profile, you will find the **Suggestion Box**. Use it to:

- Suggest a **new restaurant** that should be on the list.
- Report a **correction** (wrong address, closed location, etc.).
- Request a **new city**.
- Send **general** feedback.

Pick a category, type your suggestion, and tap **Submit**. You can submit 1 suggestion per day. The counter at the top shows how many you have left.

### What Vouch stores

- Your email and display name (for your profile and comments).
- Your votes, saved restaurants, and suggestion history.
- Your notification preferences.

### Pulling down to refresh

On the home screen and city ranking pages, pull down to refresh the data.
